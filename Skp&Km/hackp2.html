Cyber Security

Awareness of best practices for securing personal information, passwords, and devices from unauthorized access or hacking attempts. This involves using strong passwords, enabling two-factor authentication, and keeping software up to date.

Privacy Awareness

Understanding the importance of protecting one's personal information online and being aware of privacy settings on social media platforms and other online services.

Digital Literacy

Having the skills to effectively navigate and utilize digital devices, software applications, and online resources. This includes basic computer skills, internet literacy, and the ability to critically evaluate digital information.
